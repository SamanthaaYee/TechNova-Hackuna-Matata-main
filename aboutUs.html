About Us

The Problem


People who live in rural areas or Indigenous reserves may not have access to adequate technology to support their business, communicate with family, gain access to online resources, and support their children, whose education is being impacted due to the lack of technological access.


Our Process


Once the donation form is filled, our team will pick up the technology at your doorstep. The tech will be wiped of all data to ensure the security of our donors. Our team will travel to certain communities that have requested the tech and deliver it to their homes.
